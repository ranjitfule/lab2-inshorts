Kharghar: A Paradise in Navi Mumbai

short by Apaar Sharma / 03:25 pm on 28 Oct 2021,Thursday

Mumbai Police have summoned BJP worker Manish Bhanushali who claimed he tipped off the NCB about the drug party on a cruise. Bhanushali was seen holding Arbaaz Merchantt's hand after the raid and is an NCB witness. Another witness Prabhakar Sail alleged the NCB and private detective KP Gosavi asked for ₹25 crore from Shah Rukh Khan to release Aryan.
